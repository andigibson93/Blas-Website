About Us

About Luis and Allison

The BLAS Center originated from Cuenca, Ecuador. Co- Founder & CEO Luis Aucapina was born in Ecuador and was baptized in the Church of Saint Blas. As a teenager, Luis showed an interest in music and film and throughout the years Luis used the BLAS name as one of his music entities.

From 2013-2015, Luis began producing video content for various nonprofit agencies as part of his Social work profession where he met his wife and CO Founder of BLAS Center Productions, Allison Arias.

Luis and Allison worked together in the field of social services with a focus on community organizing and workforce development. During this same time they created BLAS Center Productions.
